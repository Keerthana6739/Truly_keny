eLearning Portal

eLearning Portal

Never stop learning with the Truly Kenya eLearning Portal – an educational app that opens doors for all Kenyans to gain knowledge and skills. We believe education should be accessible to everyone, so we've packed this portal with resources for students, professionals, and lifelong learners alike. Find courses on everything from the school curriculum (KCPE, KCSE materials) to vocational training, tech skills, languages, and even cultural heritage lessons. Whether you're catching up on classwork in Kisumu or learning Swahili from London, Truly Kenya keeps you on track.

Why the eLearning Portal?

Because empowering minds empowers the nation. Our eLearning platform lets you learn at your own pace, on your own schedule. Enjoy interactive video lessons, quizzes, and assignments that make learning engaging. Connect with qualified Kenyan tutors and mentors – including experts from our diaspora – for guidance and support. Earn certificates for completed courses that you can show off to employers or schools. It's a complete classroom experience, right on your device.

Key Features

Diverse Courses

Access a broad range of courses and tutorials: academic subjects (Math, Science, History), professional development (IT, business, entrepreneurship), and personal enrichment (health, finance, languages).

Interactive Learning

Watch video lectures, take practice quizzes, and participate in discussion forums. Our platform makes online learning active and enjoyable.

Local & Global Educators

Learn from the best. Courses are taught by Kenyan teachers and industry experts, with guest instructors from the diaspora bringing global perspectives.

Progress Tracking & Certification

Keep track of your progress with dashboards and earn certificates or badges for completing courses and skills.

Youth & Adult Programs

Resources for all ages – from revision material for pupils and students to career advancement courses for working adults.
