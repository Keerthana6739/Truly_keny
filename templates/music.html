Truly Kenya Music

Immerse yourself in the rich sounds of Kenya with Truly Kenya Music. This is your dedicated music streaming app celebrating Kenyan talent and the tunes you love. From afro-pop hits and gospel choruses to traditional folk songs and the latest club tracks, Truly Kenya Music brings a world of melody to your fingertips. It's designed for music lovers both in Kenya and across the globe – now the Kenyan diaspora can stay in tune with home like never before.

Why Truly Kenya Music?

It's not just another music app; it's a platform for our artists and our culture. Explore a vast library of songs across genres, with a special spotlight on Kenyan musicians and bands. Create your own playlists for every mood or vibe, and discover new favorites through personalized recommendations. Unlike generic streaming services, Truly Kenya Music reinvests in local talent – by streaming here, you're directly supporting Kenyan artists as they earn from every play.

Key Features

Extensive Music Library

Stream tens of thousands of tracks, including Kenyan hits, African classics, and international favorites – all in one place.

Personalized Playlists

Get curated playlists and recommendations tailored to your taste, from Swahili ballads to the latest gengetone anthems.

Offline Listening

Going offline or upcountry? Download your favorite songs to listen without internet, so the music never stops.

Share & Connect

Share songs or playlists with friends on Truly Kenya, and see what others are listening to. Music is more fun together!

Support Kenyan Artists

Each stream on our platform helps Kenyan creators earn revenue and grow their fanbase, strengthening the local music industry.

Listen Now

On Truly Kenya Music tune into the heartbeat of Kenyan music and let the rhythms inspire you.
