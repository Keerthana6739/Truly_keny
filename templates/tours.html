Virtual Tours & Safari Parks

Explore Kenya's wonders from anywhere in the world with Truly Kenya Virtual Tours. Can't make it to the Maasai Mara this year? No problem – we'll bring the safari to you. Our virtual tourism feature lets you embark on breathtaking journeys through Kenya's national parks, iconic landscapes, and cultural landmarks, all through your device. It's perfect for those planning a trip, educating kids about our wildlife, or for diaspora Kenyans yearning for a taste of home. You can almost feel the savannah breeze as you watch elephants graze at sunset or take a guided tour of historical sites in Nairobi.

Why Virtual Tours?

Because Kenya's beauty deserves to be experienced by all, without limits. With Truly Kenya's virtual tours, you get high-quality 360° panoramas, live camera feeds, and interactive guides at your fingertips. Enjoy narrated tours by expert guides who share fascinating facts about our wildlife and heritage. Switch between different parks and sites – one minute you're at Amboseli watching elephants by Mt. Kilimanjaro, the next you're diving into the coral reefs of Watamu Marine Park, all from the comfort of your home. It's not just sightseeing; it's about fostering appreciation for Kenya's natural and cultural treasures.

Key Features

Immersive 360° Experiences

Take virtual safaris in 360-degree view. Walk through Nairobi National Park, climb Mount Kenya, or roam the Maasai Mara, virtually as if you're there.

Live Wildlife Cams

Tune into live streams from select safari parks and reserves to see real-time wildlife action – lions, giraffes, zebras and more, in their natural habitat.

Guided Tours & Commentary

Choose guided tour modes with audio commentary in English (and local languages) to learn about each location's history, animals, and culture as you explore.

Interactive Learning

Click on points of interest to get more info, view photos, or take fun quizzes about Kenyan wildlife and heritage – great for students and curious minds.

Plan Your Trip

Like what you see? Save your favorite spots and get information on visiting them in person (nearby accommodations, travel tips, and deals) when you're ready to travel.
