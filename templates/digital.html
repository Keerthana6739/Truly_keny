Truly Kenya Digital

Truly Kenya Digital is the heart of our platform – a homegrown social media and content hub made by Kenyans, for Kenyans. Here you can share your stories, videos, music, and ideas on Kenya’s first homegrown social network​. It's a vibrant online community where creativity and conversation thrive. Whether you're a student in Mombasa or a Kenyan living abroad, this is your space to connect with others, stay informed, and celebrate what it means to be Kenyan.

Why Choose us ?

Why Truly Kenya Digital? It's not just about socializing – it's about opportunity. Content creators can earn a real income through fair content monetization: our model lets you keep up to 70% of the revenue your content generates​ far more than typical platforms.

And for everyone else, engaging with content can be rewarding too. Watch brief Educational Ads (EduAds) that teach useful tips and get rewarded for watching – you can even earn money each month just by viewing sponsored content​. Truly Kenya Digital turns your social media time into a chance to learn, earn, and shine.
