Why Choose Truly Kenya Connectivity?

It's connectivity on your terms. Through our app, you can complete a quick KYC (Know Your Customer) verification and choose a plan that fits your needs. Pay securely via mobile money or card and get your household or business connected in no time.

A personal dashboard lets you monitor your data usage and subscription status at a glance. Need to take a break or going on holiday? No problem – you can activate or deactivate your subscription with a simple tap, giving you total control over your internet service.
