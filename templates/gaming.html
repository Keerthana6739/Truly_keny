Truly Kenya Gaming

Level up your fun with Truly Kenya Gaming, a one-stop gaming platform for the Kenyan community. Whether you're a casual gamer or an e-sports enthusiast, we've got you covered. Play a variety of games right through the app – from puzzles and trivia to sports and adventure – or watch live streams of gaming events featuring Kenyan talent. Truly Kenya Gaming is where competition meets community, bringing together players from all over the country (and our diaspora) to connect and compete.

Why Truly Kenya Gaming?

Because gaming is more fun when it's shared. Our platform is designed to nurture a vibrant gaming culture in Kenya. Challenge your friends or make new ones in multiplayer matches and tournaments. Climb the leaderboards to earn recognition, and take part in special events or e-sport competitions with real prizes up for grabs. We also spotlight Kenyan game developers and local content, giving homegrown games a place to shine. It's entertainment, community, and opportunity rolled into one.

Key Features

Diverse Game Library

Enjoy a range of games – arcade, puzzle, sports, strategy, and more – accessible instantly without separate downloads.

Multiplayer & Tournaments

Play head-to-head with friends or enter nationwide tournaments. Win points, prizes, or bragging rights in events held regularly.

Live Streams & Esports

Watch top players and gaming influencers stream matches live. Cheer on Kenyan e-sports teams and learn new tricks from experts.

Leaderboards & Achievements

Track your scores, earn badges, and see how you rank against others across Kenya. Level up your profile as you play.

Developer Spotlight

Discover indie games and innovations by Kenyan developers featured in the platform, supporting our local gaming industry.
